English

I'm Sun Hsiao Yuan, and my English name is Joy. I have over five years of experience working as a video editor and filmmaker. My work spans commercial photography, video filmmaking, wedding films, script planning, and motion graphics production.

I created a travel show in collaboration with the Mayor of Taipei, where I was responsible for storyboard planning, filming, and leading the entire post-production process, including editing, sound design, and color correction.

I turn short video clips into smooth and engaging stories using Adobe Premiere Pro, and bring visuals to life with motion graphics in After Effects. Recently, I’ve been studying Python programming and improving my English skills to expand my creative possibilities.
